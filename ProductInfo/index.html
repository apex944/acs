Conical, or cone nuts, use a 60 degree taper and are popular with most aftermarket wheels, most racing wheels, and many OE wheels as well. Available for 12 mm and 14 mm studs.

R13 and R14 Spherical Seat Nuts

M12.DE.SPH
M12.US.SPH

M14.DE.SPH
M14.US.SPH

Spherical, or ball seat nuts, have a radius ground into them to match the OEM wheel nut or bolt on cars such as VW, Audi, and Porsche. It is very important to match your OE wheel seat style with the proper radius nuts.
