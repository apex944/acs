Conical, or "cone nuts" use a 60° taper on them and are popular with most aftermarket wheels, most racing wheels and many OE wheels as well. Available for 12 & 14mm studs.

R13 & R14 Spherical Seat Nuts
M12.DE.SPH
M12.US.SPH

M14.DE.SPH
M14.US.SPH

Spherical, or ball seat nuts have a radius ground into them to match the OEM wheel nut/bolt on cars such as VW, Audi and Porsche. It is very important to match the radius of your OE type wheel seats with the proper radius nuts.

M14.CU.CON

Copper Coated racing wheel nuts from ACS. The thin copper coating provides an anti-seize surface for use in even the most demanding endurance environments. The coating also "bites" the wheel seats & threads preventing nuts from loosening on track. Used by professional race teams.
