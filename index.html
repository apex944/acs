"Get On Track" with Apex Competition...

Apex Competition Systems' focus is on improving the driving experience of sports cars and racing cars through the manufacture and sale of aftermarket and competition products for maximum performance.

Apex Competition Systems brings 20 years of experience in professional and amateur road racing with organizations such as SCCA, Grand-Am, World-Challenge, and ALMS / IMSA.

Our primary products are our "Track-Stud" custom designed steel wheel studs and wheel nuts specifically engineered for use in competition. These studs and nuts have been developed with the help of racing teams competing at all levels of motorsport. From SCCA to Grand-Am our studs have been proven to perform.
