    MCS 1WNR

    A single-adjustable non-remote damper. Our heavy hitting entry level system.
    • Monotube Design
    • 18 clicks of rebound adjustment
    • 22mm strut shafts
    • 16mm shock shafts
    ORDER MCS 1W NR

    MCS 2WNR

    A double-adjustable damper with a unique adjustment mechanism for easy access and fast changes. Compression and rebound damping are adjusted independently from the shaft-side of the damper.
    • Monotube Design
    • 14 clicks of high speed compression adjustment
    • 18 clicks of rebound adjustment
    • 22mm strut shafts
    • 16mm shock shafts
    Order MCS 2W NR

    MCS 2W

    A double-adjustable damper with a remote reservoir increasing compression blowoff capacity and adjustable gas pressure. Effective stroke is increased and valving options are increased.
    • Monotube Design
    • 18 clicks of high speed compression adjustment
    • 18 clicks of rebound adjustment
    • 22mm strut and shock shafts
    • Adjustable reservoir nitrogen pressure
    ORDER MCS 2W

    MCS 3W

    Sharing components with the 2W system, the 3W system introduces low speed compression bleed allowing the user to manipulate pitch & roll more effectively with fewer compromises.
    • Monotube Design
    • 18 clicks of high speed compression adjustment
    • 10 clicks of low speed compression adjustment
    • 18 clicks of rebound adjustment
    • 22mm strut and shock shafts
    • Adjustable reservoir nitrogen pressure
    ORDER MCS 3W products

    MCS 4W

    Our most focused damper with completely independent high speed and low speed circuits for both compression and rebound damping. The operating range is narrowed to allow for more definition across each sweep of adjustments.
    • 18 clicks of high speed compression adjustment
    • 10 clicks of low speed compression adjustment
    • 18 clicks of high speed rebound adjustment
    • 10 clicks of low speed rebound adjustment
    • Adjustable reservoir nitrogen pressure
