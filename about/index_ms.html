About Us

SINCE 1964

We have set the pace for worldwide distribution of

high-performance automotive components

At Motor State, we recognize that there are many sources for aftermarket performance parts, which is why we strive to give you the excellent service and competitive pricing that inspires our customers to come back to us again and again – and recommend us to others. We work with customers across the performance spectrum, from machine shops and fabricators to chassis and engine builders, from speed shops to auto parts resellers and dealers of all sizes.﻿

Join the Motor State Team!

Motor State Distributing offers a competitive salary and a comprehensive benefits package, including a 401K program with company match. A completely smoke-free workplace, our state-of-the-art facility is designed with a focus on safety and ergonomics.
